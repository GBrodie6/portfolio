How I actually build

The parts of the work that don't show up in a screenshot.

Robotics

I'm captain and lead engineer of my high school's robotics team, running the design process end to end, from CAD and prototyping through to competition day. It's where I learned to treat a build like a system, not a one-off.

Systems, not just screens

Once a platform touches real payments, the interesting engineering stops being visible. Building GolfTradr meant adding advisory-lock payment idempotency to prevent double-charges, SSRF protection around outbound requests, 3D Secure enforcement, and a Cloudflare WAF layer with bot-fight mode and rate limiting to keep the platform standing under abuse. None of it makes a good screenshot. All of it is the actual engineering.
